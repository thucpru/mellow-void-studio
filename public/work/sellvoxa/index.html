Sellvoxa is an embeddable voice AI agent that turns any website into a proactive sales rep, built for businesses and sellers who want their site to greet, advise, and convert — with a single line of script.

About Sellvoxa

Sellvoxa — selling through voice — is an agent crafted for websites that should do more than wait. Designed for sellers, service businesses, and online stores, it gives a static page a voice, a presence, and the instinct to speak first.

Built for teams who don't have engineers to spare, Sellvoxa drops in with one line of script and starts working from the very first visitor.


A Website That Speaks First, <br>Designed to Convert

Sellvoxa was conceived as an agent that leads the conversation rather than waiting to be asked. Every interaction is intentional — greeting at the right moment, answering in real time, and guiding toward action — balancing a natural, human feel with full brand control.

Live in Three Steps

Sellvoxa needs no technical team. Paste a single `<script>` line into your page — no source-code changes, no backend — and the assistant learns your entire website, then begins to talk.

It reads your pages, products, and documents to understand your business, then builds a proactive conversation script that fits, answering only from your own knowledge — which you can update anytime.

A Voice That Feels Human

Sellvoxa talks by voice in real time, with low latency that feels like speaking to a real consultant. It listens, replies, and detects your visitor's language automatically — Vietnamese, English, and more — switching between them mid-conversation without a pause.

Instead of waiting to be questioned, it greets visitors at the right moment, with a tailored opening for each page.


Guided Toward Action

Sellvoxa doesn't just answer — it moves people forward. It scrolls the page, opens the right section, and fills in forms (only when permitted) to carry visitors to the cart, the buy button, or the sign-up form.

You stay fully in control: toggle each permission, set per-session cost limits, and manage knowledge — transparent and brand-safe by design.

A Channel That Works Around the Clock

Sellvoxa doesn't just make a strong first impression — it never clocks out. Serving 24/7, it captures the after-hours visitors a passive site would lose, converting from the traffic you already have.


Designed to Engage, Built to Sell

Every part of Sellvoxa works toward one outcome: a website that serves and persuades on its own. It gives businesses a proactive voice while keeping them in command — the result is a site that feels alive, helpful, and ready to close. A Hithuc original.
